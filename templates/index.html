Cooking-It

A place to have fun cooking

We proudly present Cooking-It! The website that is here to inspire, create joy and find people who share the same interest in food that so many have.

Our Story

We exist to create community among several individuals, who in our opinion started already during the start of the pandemic when the need for something to do in connection with others without meeting physically grew strong. Cooking then became more of a interest and many therefore turned to a lot of different sites to find inspiration for what to cook for the day. That's why we came up with the idea of creating a platform that holds several recipes and that makes it even easier for applicants. Making it no longer a necessity to jump from website to website, as this website has already sampled a lot of recipes for you.

How it works

When you create an account on Cooking-It, your own profile is included, this profile gives you access to detailed information about the website and what can be good to think about while using it. It also offers a guide on how to go about adding a recipe and has a logout button at the bottom of the page that makes it easy for you to logout from your profile. Then just look around the page to do what you are most interested in. If you use the menu at the top of the page, you will find all the pages available that will take you where you want to go. Of course it sounds exciting, scroll down a bit more and you can read about everything the website has to offer. If you already feel compelled, click on the button below to create your very own profile.

What You Get

It's not just about food, it's about taking part and sharing what everyone has to offer. At least that's what we think. To begin with, as you may already know, you are offered a profile by creating an account. In this account you can read recipes that already exist, comment on them if you feel you want to try them, read other people's comments and if necessary also delete the comment you have already posted. In addition to that, you can add your own recipe yourself by following the menu at the top of the page or clicking on the button that is already in your profile, it's entirely up to you. All we wish for is that you find joy in this website and have fun while cooking.
