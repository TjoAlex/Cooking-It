Cooking-It

A place to have fun cooking

We proudly present Cooking-It! The website that is here to inspire, create joy and find people who share the same interest in food that so many have.

Our Story

We exist to create community among several individuals, who in our opinion start already during the start of the pandemic when the need for something to do in connection with others without meeting physically grew strong. Cooking then became more of a interest and many therefore turned to a lot of different sites to find inspiration for what to cook for the day. That's why we came up with the idea of creating a platform that holds several recipes and that makes it even easier for applicants. Making it no longer a necessity to jump from website to website, as this website has already sampled a lot of recipes for you.

How it works

You must start by creating an account or logging in directly to your existing account in order to use the site to its full potential. With just a few simple steps, you can create your account and we promise that it will not take more than a few minutes before you get started. Click the buttons below to create an account or proceed by following the menu at the top of the page. Furthermore, you can then find recipes that are already on the page or add your own recipes that you already have and can think of sharing with others. Again, follow the menu at the top of the page to find your way around the website. For further information about how the website works, scroll down the page and you will also find a video that gives a detailed tour.

What You Get

When you create an account on Cooking-It, your own profile is included, which collects all your recipes you have posted. It provides the ability to edit all your recipes and or delete them. You will have access to other people's recipes that are on the website and also a chance to post a review of the recipe you tried by someone else. Furthermore, you can also make sure to heart the recipes you like so that when there is time you have the opportunity to find in your profile the recipes you liked/heart and later try them without them disappearing. In addition to all this, you can also use the search function located at the top of the recipe page, which makes it easy for you to search for recipes based on category or other things. Everything mentioned above is free for all and you can be safe knowing that if ever a cost would be needed for anything, clear information about this will be provided and nothing will ever be forced to pay, only offered for either support or purchases one would like to do.
